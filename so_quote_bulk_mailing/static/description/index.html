SO/QUOTATION MASS MAILING

In this module you can send bulk mail for sale orders and quotations.

Step 1:

Select your records that you want to send mail.

Step 2:

And now click on Action and click the "Send SO/Quote by Mail" button.

Step 3:

Once you click the button "Send SO/Quote by Mail", you can see the wizard view and you can also add or delete the records if you want.

Step 4:

Then click the "Send Mail" button to send the mail for the selected records.
